BUDALK CARBONARA RAMEN

Another simple meal is packaged ramen, my favorite ever is Budalk Ramen! Here's my classic recipe to make it into perfection

DIRECTIONS

First, follow the instructions on the ramen packet. Please cook with a pan and stove lide. Once the noodles are fully cooked, you want to take out the water and replace it with milk.
During the same time, soft boil the eggs. That would consist of boiling a pot of water and placing the eggs in there for seven minutes.
After you mixed the sauce packets into the ramen, you would want to add cheese and scallions on top and let it cook for a minute more with the lid on.
Put on a plate, garnish with a more scallions and enjoy!
Budalk Carbonara Ramen
Five Tablespoons of Milk
Two Eggs
One handful of Green Onions
Half a cup of Cheese of your choice
