EGG O MAMA

A gift from my mom.

I've been making this meal for as long as I can remember. I usually eat it when I'm sick and don't have the energy to make anything else, or I make it when I'm missing home. Believe me, I make this meal a unhealthy amount of times. Especially now when I'm the only family that lives in the east coast, while literally everyone else is in the west coast. It can get exhasutingly lonely, and when you just want to see your family, this is such a healing meal.

This is what you'll need!

  • About 2 cups of Rice
  • 1 tablespoon of Soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Seaseme Oil
  • Egg
  • Scallions
  • Seaseme Seeds

Here's what you'll need to do!

  1. Get a cup full of steaming rice into a bowl.
  2. Get an egg and remove the whites, OR fry an egg and make sure you do it sunny side up.
  3. Put the yolk of the egg in the rice, or the sunny side up egg.
  4. Now, add in the soysauce and seaseme oil.
  5. Mix it in the bowl. Make sure everything is incorporated.
  6. Chop up the scallions until thinly sliced and top with seaseme seeds.
  7. Enjoy!.
