RED BULL ICEE

This has been my life line when it comes to my college experience. During one of my late plunges into doing my assignments, I craved something cold and sweet. Due to my cravins, I created a monstrosity which housed all my fears in my graphic design talents and skills. I call it, the Red Bull Icee.

Choose your pick.

Wether on the flavor, you just need to grab a handful of ICE, REDBULL and FIVE HOUR ENERGY. Put all of these ingredients in a blender, and serve with some lemon juice! Ignore the loud pounding in your heart afterwards.
