ARMY STEW

My Childhood Meal

My father made his own version of Army Stew using a ramen packet.
He promised me he would show me how to make it but he never did.
So I'm just guesstimating here. My father was always the better cook
in the family I think I got my cooking skills from my mom.

Here is What You'll Need!

  • Water
  • Shin Ramen (The whole thing)
  • Gochujang
  • Bouillion Powder
  • Pork Belly (any meat will do)
  • Kimchi and Kimchi Juice
  • Green Peppers
  • Onions
  • Scallions
Again, I eyeballed the porportions, so your hand will
naturally tell you when there's enough of each thing.

Instructions

  1. Fill a pot half way with water and begin to boil it. If you think you
    haven't put enough water in, don't worry, you haven't.
  2. Cut up the vegetables, the green peppers, the onions and the scallions.
  3. Add the kimchi juice, the gochujang, the beef bouillion powder
    and the shin ramen soup packet into the water now. Let it boil.
  4. Now, add in the green peppers (make sure the seeds are out) and onions.
  5. Add the pork belly.
  6. Add the ramen noodles in finally and then add the scallions.
  7. Wait until noodles are soft and serve. Eat with rice.
