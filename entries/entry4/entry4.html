KIMCHI FRIED RICE

My Favorite Meal

When it comes to everything I have made, I have probably made this the most. Unfortunately, I can't do that quite as much now that I don't have a rice cooker, but I can always remisince for the sake of reminiscing.

This is what you'll need!

  • A lot of Rice
  • A lot of Kimchi and Kimchi Juice
  • A dallop of Seaseme Oil
  • One Egg
  • 4 strips of chopped Bacon
  • Half a Onion Onions
  • Table Spoon of Gochujang
  • Table Spoon of Sugar
  • An Egg.. if you feel like it!

Here's what you'll need to do!

  1. Fry your bacon and LEAVE the fat in the pan.
  2. Chop up some onions to your perferred length and add them in.
  3. Add in your Kimchi, and fry it for a bit out a minute, before putting in the juice.
  4. Now add the rice, sugar, and a table spoon of gochujang..
  5. Fry the rice in however way you please, drizzling in the seaseme oil to add nuttiness.
  6. Serve in plate, fry an add an egg on top!
  7. Enjoy!.
