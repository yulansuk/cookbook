hwachae

Even though it's fall, it sure feels like summer. NYC, please cool down oh my god. Walking around these streets, it really wants a refreshing beverage, and nothing is more refereshing then hwachae!

ingredients

Strawberry Milk
A Watermelon
Jelly and Sago
Ice

let's make it

  1. Take a watermelon and cut it in half
  2. Scoop up the guts and cut it up
  3. Re add the chopped up watermelon back in the bowl
  4. Add the jelly, sago and ice
  5. Pour the strawberry milk into the watermelon bowl
  6. Enjoy!
