Want something salty and.. really oily?

Imagine this, it's 10pm, you're extremely tired and you just want a meal you can slap on the stove, shake around a bit in the pan and eat before bed.
I got the perfect meal for you, and it only takes like, ten minutes.

Here is what you'll need...

  • Firm Tofu
  • Pork Belly
  • White Vinegar
  • Kimchi with the Juice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Seaseme Oil
  • Gochujang
  • Scallions
  • Vegetable Oil
    • Porportions size doesn't matter because I just eyeballed them.

    Step 1!

    Put like, two spoonfuls of oil into the pan and heat it up. When the pan is steaming, you know it's ready.

    Step 2!

    Chop up the pork belly and the tofu into one inch squares, and fry them in the skillet for a bit

    Step 3!

    Seperate the kimchi from the juice and add the kimchi into the pan and let it fry for a bit.

    Step 4!

    Add the juice, soy sauce, rice vinegar and the seaseme oil. Fry until the tofu and meat is saturated red!

    Serve in dish and eat with rice!
