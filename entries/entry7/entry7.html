SPAM MUSUBI

This is my go to lazy lazy meal! It's delicious and easy to make! The clean up is also rather simple too.

STEPS

  1. Rinse 1 cup of rice until the water is clear.
  2. Combine the rice and 1/2 cup water in a rice cooker or pot. Cook according to instructions (about 15-20 minutes)
  3. Slice the spam into 1/4 to 1/2 inch pieces.
  4. Fry the spam in a pan until golden brown on both sides (about 3-4 minutes each side).
  5. Mix 2 tablespoons soy sauce and 1 tablespoon sugar in a bowl.
  6. Pour the sauce over the fried spam and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Take a portion of cooked rice (about 1/4 cup) and press it down on a seaweed sheet.
  8. Place a slice of spam on top of the rice.
  9. Add another layer of rice and press down.
  10. Wrap the seaweed around the rice and spam.
